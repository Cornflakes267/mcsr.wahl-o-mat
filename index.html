MCSR Wahl-O-Mat

15 speedrunners have answered the statements. Now it's your turn: compare your points of view with the speedrunners' answers

This is inspired by the German Wahl-O-Mat that determines which parties represent your political views the best based on statements you and the parties have answered.

Note that choosing "Neutral" and "Skip Statement" has two different effects:
"Neutral" means that you both consider pro and con arguments as valid and can't decide for one side. This statement will still be considered in your results.
"Skip Statement" means that you don't have an opinion about the statement. This statement will not be considered in your results.

At the end, you will see all the questions you have answered again.
If one topic is especially important to you, you can check the box to weigh it twice as important as other statements.
